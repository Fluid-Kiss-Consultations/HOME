## What FKC Is

**Fluid Kiss Consultations** is a Systems Oracle — full-service consulting that sees entire systems (organizational, technical, economic, cultural), diagnoses where they fracture, and rebuilds from the intersection of every discipline involved.

FKC does not consult in silos. The founder's background spans master trades, technology, psychology, sociology, business, history, and comparative systems analysis. The diagnostic methodology is adaptive cross-domain synthesis — a technology problem is a people problem is an economics problem is a structural design failure. Every engagement benefits from this integrated perspective.

### Positioning

- **Who we serve:** Mid-market to enterprise organizations needing systems-level transformation, plus mission-driven organizations and nonprofits through tiered pricing.
- **What we sell:** Strategic diagnostics, compliance architecture, DevSecFinOps, operational optimization, full blockchain platforms, org+tech transformation, and ongoing advisory/CTO retainers.
- **How we price:** Three tiers — Standard, Mission-Aligned (75%), Solidarity (60%). The work doesn't change. The access does.

### Brand Voice

- Direct, confident, zero-jargon
- Speaks to decision-makers, not procurement departments
- Warmth without softness — the tone of someone who's built the thing, run the thing, and knows why it breaks
- Never begs for attention — states what it does and why it matters

## Conventions for AI Assistants

1. **Read before writing.** Always read FKC-SITE-PLAN.md before suggesting content changes.
2. **Content source of truth.** The site plan in `doc/` is canonical. Update the plan first, then apply to the builder.
3. **Brand voice consistency.** Direct, confident, zero-jargon. Review the Brand Voice section above before writing any copy.
4. **Ecosystem awareness.** This repo represents the public face of FKC and the Blinded Eye Foundation. Content about B.R.A.N.D.I., S.H.A.N.N.O.N., and N.I.K.O.System should be accurate but accessible — don't dump internal architecture on the marketing site.
5. **Pricing is public.** The three-tier model is intentionally transparent. Don't hedge it.
6. **Motto is canon.** "Innovate. Elevate. Inspire." — use it consistently, in that order.
7. **Copyright.** All content falls under: © 2024 John A. Welch, Director, Blinded Eye Foundation.
